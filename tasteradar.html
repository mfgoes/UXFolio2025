The Market Opportunity

Group dining decisions create analysis paralysis, with 73% of groups spending over 15 minutes choosing restaurants, often defaulting to familiar chains.

As co-founder and Head of Product, I identified an opportunity to simplify local food discovery through personalized curation. While platforms like Google Maps and Yelp offer comprehensive listings, they lack intelligent filtering for group preferences and local gem discovery. Our solution: an AI-powered platform that presents exactly three curated options based on group dynamics and individual taste profiles.

Product Strategy & Market Positioning

Built a defensible product strategy focused on curation over information overload:

Three-Option Rule

Eliminated decision fatigue through curated recommendations

Based on choice overload research showing 3 options optimize decision-making

Local-First Algorithm

Prioritized independent restaurants over chains

Differentiated from Google Maps through discovery of hidden gems

Group Intelligence

Factored in group dynamics and shared preferences

Solved for collective decision-making, not individual choice

Product Development & User Research

Behavioral Research

Conducted 45+ user interviews revealing that group dining decisions took 15+ minutes on average, with 60% defaulting to familiar options due to choice overload.

Prototype Validation

Built and tested MVP with 500+ beta users in Amsterdam, achieving 4.2/5 recommendation satisfaction and 65% weekly retention.

Algorithm Optimization

Implemented machine learning recommendation engine using dining history, dietary preferences, and group composition to improve match accuracy.

Key Product Features

Smart Onboarding & Personalization

Designed a progressive onboarding that captured taste preferences without overwhelming users. Integrated Google SSO for frictionless signup while building comprehensive user profiles through implicit feedback and dining behavior.

  • Dietary preference mapping with 85% accuracy improvement over generic filters
  • Group creation tools for shared decision-making
  • Real-time availability integration with restaurant partners
  • Net Promoter Score tracking for continuous recommendation improvement

Go-to-Market Strategy

Led comprehensive go-to-market strategy focusing on community building and strategic partnerships in the Netherlands food scene.

  • Secured partnerships with sustainable food brands (Willicroft, Soosee, Plant Based Fashion)
  • Built influencer network with food-focused Instagram accounts reaching 100K+ combined followers
  • Implemented viral referral system increasing organic user acquisition by 40%
  • Created content strategy highlighting local restaurant stories and chef interviews
