The Challenge

PostNL's "become a business customer" flow had a 73% drop-off rate, losing potential clients to DHL, DPD, and integrator apps like MyParcel and SendCloud.

With limited development capacity, we needed high-impact changes that would reduce massive support overhead costs. The existing process required weeks of manual setup, while competitors offered self-service onboarding in days. Small clients were the lowest value but highest cost due to support volume - enabling self-service plugin setup (Shopify, WooCommerce) was critical for profitability.

Research & Strategy

In-Field Research

Visited clients in their natural environments - offices and warehouses - to understand real PostNL software usage and onboarding pain points. This revealed the gap between designed experience and actual user behavior.

Competitive Analysis

DHL, DPD offered self-service onboarding in days. Integrator apps like MyParcel and SendCloud were winning small clients with instant setup. We needed to match their speed.

Support Cost Analysis

Small clients generated 80% of support tickets but only 20% of revenue. Self-service plugin setup was identified as the key to flipping this equation and reducing overhead.

From Insights to Strategy

Field research and cost analysis revealed clear priorities. I led multiple A/B tests to validate our approach:

  • Copy optimization: Tested technical vs. benefit-focused language, improving comprehension by 40%
  • Plugin setup flow: A/B tested wizard vs. single-page approach for Shopify integration
  • Usability testing: Validated self-service flows with 20+ small business owners
  • Progressive disclosure: Tested when to show advanced features vs. keeping setup minimal

Design Solution

Revolutionary Approach

I redesigned the entire flow from a complex 7-step process to a streamlined experience, pioneering a "one-page signup" approach that was groundbreaking for B2B onboarding at PostNL.

  • Created responsive experience for desktop and mobile web app
  • Built comprehensive design system for Belgium market expansion
  • Implemented smart routing for high-volume customers to sales teams
  • Designed intuitive product and plugin selection interfaces

Testing & Validation

I led a comprehensive testing strategy to validate our approach and ensure technical feasibility while maintaining user satisfaction.

  • A/B tested one-page vs multi-step signup approaches
  • Conducted prototype testing with real business customers
  • Collaborated closely with developers on technical feasibility
  • Measured task completion time and user satisfaction metrics
  • Created detailed roadmap of feature launches and page rollouts
