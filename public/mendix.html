Understanding the problem

Mendix is one of the leading low-code software solutions specializing in enterprise solutions. This software is used by clients such as ABN Amro, PostNL, and Silver Cross, where security is essential.

In order to prove the security of the platform, annual security checks are performed by Security Officers. These security audits tend to be a very stressful period, being tedious and time-consuming. Compliance teams had to manage certifications across multiple applications over strict deadlines, leading to:

  • Lack of Transparency – No clear overview of audit progress.
  • Inefficient Communication – Dispersed conversations across email, Slack, and tickets.
  • Manual Errors – Evidence validation was inconsistent, causing delays.

During my time in the internal apps team (BITS), we saw an opportunity to create a single platform where security officers, compliance teams, and evidence suppliers could efficiently collaborate and track audits.
